Services Details

At Hart Dental, we offer a broad spectrum of services to meet your every need. All ages are welcome, from children to adults. Below, you’ll find a menu of our many solutions that can help you have a healthier, happier smile.

General Dentistry

If you need a routine examination, cleaning, X-rays, oral cancer screening or sports mouth guards, our dentists can suit your requirements. Our range of general dentistry services will keep your teeth, gums and mouth in great shape.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Have you ever wanted to change the look of your smile? Porcelain veneers, tooth contouring, crown lengthening and teeth whitening are available to alter how your teeth appear, giving your self-confidence a boost.

Dermal Fillers & Wrinkle Relaxers

Small subtle enhancements or the disappearance of fine lines using dermal fillers & wrinkle relaxers is sometimes all you need to feel refreshed and ready to face the world. Request your free consultation and our skilled dentists will work with you to get the look you’ve always wanted.

Restorative Dentistry

If your teeth have been damaged due to trauma or aging, you require removal or would like to have a dental implant placed, our many restorative dentistry solutions may be the ideal answer. In addition to dental implant restorations, we offer crowns, bridges, fillings, Bioclear®, inlays and onlays.

Oral Surgery

Sometimes, it isn’t possible to save a tooth from extraction. In such cases, we can perform a routine extraction. We also offer wisdom teeth extraction and can place dental implants to restore your oral health.

Children’s Dentistry

We love to see children learning good oral hygiene habits from an early age. To keep your children’s teeth healthy, we offer examinations and cleanings, early orthodontic treatment, fissure sealants, fluoride treatments and smart fillings.

Sedation Dentistry

At Hart Dental, we cater to your needs while keeping your best interest in mind. An essential part of maintaining your healthy smile is making sure you’re comfortable throughout each visit. When necessary, we offer sedation dentistry options so that you can feel relaxed and we can proceed with ease.

Facial Pain & TMD Treatment

If you suffer from pain in the ear, jaw or elsewhere in the face, as well as headaches, we have conservative options to treat your problem. We’ll determine the source of your problem and recommend the necessary treatment to help you find relief.

Early Orthodontics

At Hart Dental, we believe that preventing problems early in life is the key to enjoying great oral health in adulthood. From age 5 on, potential orthodontic problems can be identified and treated.
